Lead Project
Research Springer Published

Alzheimer's Disease Classification

ML classification of Alzheimer's cognitive states from digital biomarkers — peer-reviewed and published by Springer.

Springer · AIAI 2025 Presented in Limassol, Cyprus Bio-Hermes cohort

Classified individuals into cognitive states of Alzheimer's disease using machine learning on the Bio-Hermes dataset. Employing XGBoost, Random Forest, and Monte Carlo cross-validation with rigorous feature selection, the work demonstrates high-accuracy classification from digital biomarker data, advancing clinical AI decision-support for neurodegenerative conditions. Invited to present at the AIAI 2025 International Conference in Limassol, Cyprus.
